Mogadorian

Nine Loric children were sent to Earth as the Mogadorians destroyed their planet, Lorien. Before the garde left, they were each given chests filled with their inheritance of special Loric artifacts and weapons. A protective charm was cast on them that prevents the Mogs from harming the children out of order as long as they remain apart. Now, they must live in hiding and train, waiting until the day that they develop their Legacies (powers), find the other garde, and fight the Mogadorians who are hunting them.

Pittacus Lore

Pittacus Lore is the title of one of the ten elders of Lorien. Pittacus Lore is the leader of the Elders and has access to all legacies through the legacy called Ximic. The Pittacus Lore at the time of the Mogadorian invasion of Lorien had past connections to Setrákus Ra and traveled to a human named Malcolm Goode to tell him about the Loric and what was coming.

Legacies: Ximic

Number Three

John Smith (Number Four)

John tends to think more with his heart than his head. Longing for a normal life where he and Henri can settle down, John is reluctant to leave his first real human connections. Because of his sentiment and tendency to trust people, he forms good connections with the other garde and becomes their leader, even if he is described as being in over his head.

Legacies: Lumen, Animal Telepathy, Ximic

Cêpan: Henri

Five (Number Five)

Five is a victim of circumstance. He was left alone at a young age and approached by good seeming people with malicious intent. Five's number one goal is to survive, even it means aligning himself with the enemy.

Legacies: Flight, Externa

Cêpan: Rey

Six (Number Six)

From a young age, Six was tomboyish and loved to train. She's been alone since the age of 13 when she and Katarina were captured by Mogadorians. Since then, Six has grown tough and tends to trade empathy for her cool demeanor. She is well-versed in combat and has great control over her legacies.

Legacies: Invisibility, Control of Weather

Cêpan: Katarina

Marina (Number Seven)

Marina is compassionate and gentle. She lived a very sheltered life in a convent in Spain where she had to hide her legacies and her cêpan became remiss of her duties. Because of this, she is the least experienced fighter of the remaining garde.

Legacies: Healing, Breathing under Water, Seeing in the Dark, Cryokinesis

Cêpan: Adelina

Eight (Number Eight)

Eight is charming and playful, despite living in the mountains on his own for many years after his cêpan was murdered. He has an uncannily carefree attitude that may be connected to the prophecy of his death hanging over his head. His fighting capabilities are on par with Six's and Nine's.

Legacies: Teleportation, Shapeshifting

Cêpan: Reynolds

Nine (Number Nine)

Nine is a good combat strategist and is the strongest fighter of the garde due to his intense training with Sandor. His imprisonment in a Mogadorian prison, which he blames himself for, causes him to enjoy killing Mogadorians. Nine is sarcastic and reckless, though it may be a cover for his past trauma.

Legacy: Antigravity, Animal Telepathy, Legacy Transference, Super Hearing

Cêpan: Sandor

Ella

Just a baby when Lorien was destroyed, Ella escaped on the second ship off Lorien with Crayton who worked for her father. She is an Aeternus, meaning she was born with the ability to move back and forth between ages that have already been lived.

Legacies: Dreynen

Cêpans are powerless Loric who took on bureaucratic and political roles on Lorien. Some could choose to become Mentor Cêpans who were assigned to young garde to help them to hone their Legacies and teach them about Lorien. To the nine remaining garde who are on the run and must keep their true identity hidden from everyone, their cêpans are the closest thing they have to family.

A race of aliens from the dying planet Mogadore. There are two types of Mogs: Vatborn, which are grown in vats and Trueborn, which are born of true Mogadorian heritage. The Mogadorians destroyed Lorien and hunt the remaining Loric on Earth. They are generally ruthless in serving their 'Beloved Leader' to bring about Mogadorian Progress.

Setrákus Ra

The leader of the Mogadorians. His genetic engineering and creation of the vatborn saved the dying race. The mogs commonly refer to him as their "Beloved Leader", and they follow his teachings from "The Great Book". He hunts down the garde children that stand in the way of expanding the Mogadorian dominion.

Legacies: Dreynen

Andrakkus Sutekh

A high ranking, trueborn Mogadorian soldier in charge of Ashwood Estates, a Mogadorian base in Washington DC. He lead the hunt for the first three garde children.

Adamus Sutekh

The trueborn son of General Andrakkus Sutekh. He was loyal to Mogadorian Progress and excelled in his classes, but an experiment to access One's memories turns Adam into a valuable Garde ally.

Legacy: Control of Seismic Waves

Ivanick Shu-Ra

A trueborn who was adopted by the General. He excels in physical training and has honored the Mogadorian cause by killing Two.

Rexicus Saturnus

A trueborn Mogadorian commander who's belief in the Mogadorian Cause ebbs away as he is forced to work with the traitor, Adam, to survive.

Doctor Anu

A trueborn Mogadorian scientist who designed the original machine that extracts memories from human and garde.

Doctor Zakos

The trueborn replacement for Dr. Anu. He improves upon Anu's research and assists the Beloved Leader with his experiments.
